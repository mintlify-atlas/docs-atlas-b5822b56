<Info>
  This glossary covers the core terminology used throughout *Yù Yú: Decoding Agent Harness*. Terms are sorted alphabetically and grouped into letter-range sections — click any section to expand it.

  **How to use this glossary:**
  - Each row includes the English term, its recommended Chinese translation, a concise definition, and the chapter where the term is first discussed in depth.
  - Technical terms are kept in their original English form; Chinese translations are provided for supplementary understanding.
  - For detailed information on specific feature flags, see Appendix C. For tool attribute information, see Appendix B.
